How It Works

From messy notes to
a working system.

I start with how your kitchen actually runs — not how a consultant imagines it should.

01

Ops Discovery

We map your positions, pain points, staffing model, and rush patterns. I shadow your reality, not a slide deck.

02

System Design

I write a knowledge base spec — deployment rules, break logic, risk flags — and get your sign-off before a single line of code.

03

Build & Iterate

Fast prototypes you can test on a real shift. We refine until leaders on your team can run it without you in the room.

04

Deploy & Train

Install on phones, import your roster, train shift leads, and hand off documentation. You own everything — no subscriptions required.
